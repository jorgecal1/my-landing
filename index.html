Measure

Measure your weight every day. You can lose of gain 1/2 pounds per week. If you are increating weight: take action. Eat less or spend more calories on trainningm.

Train

Train in the apropiate heart rating zone. If you traib between the 65 and 75% of you maximum heart rate, you will burn more fatm.

Test
